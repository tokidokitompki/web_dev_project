About Me!

Welcome to my introduction! My name is Taylor Tompkins.

Background

I was born in Atlanta, Georgia and have lived in the surrounding metro Atlanta region since. So, Georgia is really all I have known! I have relatives scattered all across the country though and so have visited a lot of the states. The farthest north I have traveled is to Canada and the farthest west is California. I work full-time at a hospital laboratory and I am a full-time online student. I like to think that I am a highly motivated, positive person that enjoys a lot of things: I enjoy gardening and trying new recipes and cuisines. I enjoy nature and hiking through beautiful landscapes. I enjoy video games and K-dramas/J-dramas. I adore kombucha and anything green tea related.

Experiences

I have attended 3 different schools: Gordon State College, West Georgia Technical College, and currently Kennesaw State University. I was a few credits short on an Associate's Degree in Biology at Gordon State College. I earned an Associate of Applied Science in Clinical Laboratory Technology from West Georgia Technical College. Currently at Kennesaw State University, I am almost finished on earning a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. I have experience in a few different fields (retail, service industry, call center) but the bulk of my expertise lies in the clinical laboratory science realm. I hope to soon have experience in the tech field after graduation! I truly love working in the lab - working as a team to deliver patient results to the doctors and nurses is very rewarding and there is never a dull moment. I still would like to stay within the lab world after graduation which is why I would like to work in LIS - this way I am still helping the lab team deliver patient results but behind the scenes instead.

Goals

I have many goals as there are a lot of things I wish to do in my lifetime! I do, however, have main goals that I am diligently working on achieving: Earning/Saving up enough money to buy real estate to rent out and for land to build a house. Transitioning my current career as a medical laboratory technician to working in the Laboratory Information Systems (LIS). Live and work in New Zealand for a few years with a visa. Visit Japan and South Korea to experience the food, culture, traditions and landmarks.
